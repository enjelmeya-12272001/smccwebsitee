About SMCC

Saint Michael College of Caraga, formerly Saint Michael Institute, is a private Catholic school in Nasipit, Agusan del Norte. It was established in 1948 by the Missionarii Sacratissimi Cordis (MSC) fathers. The school catered to high school students until the year 2000, when it opened its elementary and college departments. The following year, the institute was renamed to Saint Michael College of Caraga (SMCC). At the same time, the school released its Vision for 2020, "Magbayanihan Tayo", which aims for producing globally competitive learners.
